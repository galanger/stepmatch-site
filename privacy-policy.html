Privacy Policy — StepMatch

Effective date: April 18, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 18, 2026

StepMatch ("the app", "we", "us") is a fitness-gaming Android app that turns your real-world steps into in-game energy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, how it's stored, and your rights.

TL;DR: We read your step count and active calories from Health Connect to power gameplay. We store your game progress and leaderboard nickname on Firebase so you can compete globally. We do not sell your data. Camera and microphone are used only locally on your device for the optional Voice & Camera mode. You can request data deletion at any time.

3. Health Connect data — special disclosure

StepMatch reads step count and active calories burned from Android Health Connect. This is required for the core game mechanic (steps → energy).

  • Health data is read only while you grant Health Connect permission. You can revoke it anytime in Settings → Health Connect.
  • Health data is not sent to our servers. It is read on your device and immediately converted into in-game currency (coins/diamonds). Only the resulting game state is saved to Firestore.
  • We do not share your health data with third parties, use it for advertising, or sell it.
  • Health data is not used for any purpose beyond calculating in-game rewards and milestones.

4. Camera and microphone — Voice & Camera mode

Voice & Camera mode is an optional game mode. If you choose to use it:

  • The camera feed is shown on-screen in real time as your gameplay background, and is never saved, uploaded, or sent anywhere.
  • The microphone is used only to measure audio amplitude (loudness) for hands-free controls. We do not record audio, transcribe speech, or store any microphone data.
  • Both sensors are disabled when you exit this mode.
  • The permissions are declared as optional in the app manifest and can be denied without affecting the rest of the game.

5. Third-party service providers

We use the following Google Firebase services under Google's data processing terms:

  • Firebase Authentication — anonymous and Google sign-in
  • Cloud Firestore — game state and leaderboard database
  • Firebase Storage — delivery of game assets (video backgrounds, pet animations)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) — push notifications for challenges

Google's privacy practices are described at policies.google.com/privacy.

6. Data retention

  • Game progress data is retained as long as your account exists. If your account has been inactive for more than 24 months, we may delete it.
  • Leaderboard entries are public as long as your account is active.
  • Crash logs are retained by Firebase for up to 90 days.
  • You can request deletion at any time (see section 7).

7. Your rights

You can:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correct — ask us to fix inaccurate data.
  • Delete — ask us to delete your account and all associated data.
  • Revoke permissions — turn off Health Connect, camera, microphone, or notification permissions in your Android system settings at any time.
  • Object / restrict — ask us to stop or limit processing.
To exercise any of these rights, email stepmatch97@gmail.com with the subject "StepMatch Data Request". We'll respond within 30 days.

8. Security

  • All network traffic between the app and Firebase is encrypted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).
  • Firestore access is restricted by security rules that prevent users from reading or modifying other users' private data.
  • The upload keystore for the Android app is kept offline.
  • Anti-cheat bounds are enforced server-side to prevent leaderboard manipulation.

9. Children's privacy

StepMatch is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at stepmatch97@gmail.com and we will delete it.

10. International users

StepMatch is operated from Israel. If you use the app from outside Israel, you understand that your data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure, which may be located in the United States or other countries. By using StepMatch, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change. Material changes will be announced in-app or via a notification.
