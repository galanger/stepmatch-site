StepMatch

Turn every step into an adventure.

StepMatch is a fitness-gaming Android app that converts your real-world steps, read securely through Health Connect, into in-game energy for a skill-based runner. Explore themed worlds, collect pets, and climb a global leaderboard — one step at a time.

Get it on Google Play

Currently available on Android only.

What's inside

Size-matching runner

Hold to grow, release to shrink. Fit through gaps, dodge obstacles, and chain perfect passes in a pure skill-based core loop.

Three themed worlds

Run through the Aztec Temple, blast through Space, and strategize in the Chess Kingdom. New worlds unlock as you earn stars.

Infinity Mode

An endless run for when you want to push your personal best. No end screen — just you, the track, and your reflexes.

Voice & Camera Mode

Optional hands-free mode. Your camera becomes the background and your voice controls the run. Nothing is recorded or uploaded.

Collectible pets

Unlock and level up four companions: Dash the Fox, Boulder the Bear, Marina the Turtle, and Blaze the Dragon.

Global leaderboard

Compete worldwide with a leaderboard nickname. Anti-cheat bounds are enforced server-side to keep scores honest.

Badges & rewards

Earn badges, spin the lottery wheel, try the slot machine, and open step-milestone chests as you rack up distance.

Steps become energy

Your daily steps and active calories are read locally via Health Connect and turned into coins and diamonds. Walk more, play more.

How it works

  • Install StepMatch from Google Play and grant Health Connect permission.
  • Walk through your day as usual. Steps and active calories convert into in-game currency.
  • Spend that currency on runs, pets, and spins. Earn stars to unlock new worlds.
  • Post your best scores to the global leaderboard and collect badges along the way.

Privacy at a glance

Health data is read on your device and converted immediately into in-game currency — it never leaves your phone. Only your game progress and leaderboard nickname are saved to Firebase. Camera and microphone are used only locally in Voice & Camera mode and are never uploaded. See the full privacy policy for details.
