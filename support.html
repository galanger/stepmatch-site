Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my steps?

StepMatch reads your step count and active calories from Android Health Connect. On first launch, accept the Health Connect permission prompt. If you skipped it, open the StepMatch settings inside the app and grant Health Connect access, or go to your Android system settings → Health Connect → App permissions → StepMatch and enable both "Steps" and "Active calories burned".

Once connected, your daily steps are read on your device and converted into in-game coins and diamonds.

Why isn't my step count updating?

Try these in order:

  • Make sure Health Connect is installed and has recent step data (open the Health Connect app to verify).
  • Check that StepMatch has permission to read Steps and Active calories in Health Connect → App permissions.
  • Make sure battery optimization is not blocking Health Connect or your step-counting app (Google Fit, Samsung Health, etc.).
  • Close and reopen StepMatch so it re-reads your latest data.
  • If you just walked, give your phone a minute — some devices only write step data to Health Connect every few minutes.

If the issue persists, email us with your device model and Android version.

How do I reset my progress?

There is no one-tap reset inside the app, because progress is tied to your leaderboard entry. If you want to start over, email stepmatch97@gmail.com from the account you use in the app and ask for a progress reset. We will clear your coins, diamonds, stars, badges, and pet state.

How do I delete my account?

Email stepmatch97@gmail.com with the subject "StepMatch Data Request" and ask for account deletion. We will remove your game progress, leaderboard entry, nickname, friend data, and push token from Firebase. We respond to deletion requests within 30 days. See the privacy policy for more detail on what is stored and how.

Why does the app need camera and microphone permissions?

Both are used only for the optional Voice & Camera mode:

  • The camera shows a live video feed as your gameplay background. It is never saved or uploaded.
  • The microphone measures audio amplitude (loudness) for hands-free controls. We do not record audio or transcribe speech.

Both permissions are optional. You can deny them and still play the rest of the game normally. They are disabled as soon as you exit Voice & Camera mode.

How do I add friends?

Open the Social section in the app and send a friend request using your friend's leaderboard nickname. Once they accept, you can see each other's progress and send challenge invites. Friend and group data is only visible to you and the people in that group.

Can I play offline?

Core runner gameplay works offline, but some features require a connection:

  • Global leaderboard updates, friend challenges, and push notifications need internet.
  • Saving progress to the cloud needs internet. If you play offline, your latest run will sync the next time you connect.
  • Some video backgrounds and pet animations are downloaded the first time you use them.
How do I earn diamonds?

Diamonds are earned from:

  • Active calories burned, read from Health Connect — the harder your activity, the more you earn.
  • Step milestone chests, which unlock as you hit step thresholds.
  • The lottery wheel and slot machine, which can pay out diamonds as a prize.
  • Badges, which grant a one-time diamond reward when unlocked.
How do I unlock new worlds?

New worlds unlock by collecting stars. Play the Aztec Temple to earn stars, then unlock Space, then the Chess Kingdom. Stars are awarded based on your performance in each run.

Which pets are available and how do I get them?

There are four collectible pets: Dash the Fox, Boulder the Bear, Marina the Turtle, and Blaze the Dragon. They are unlocked through gameplay progression and in-game currency. Each pet has its own look and can be equipped during runs.

Is StepMatch available on iPhone?

Not yet. StepMatch is currently Android only, distributed through Google Play.
